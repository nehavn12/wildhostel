Welcome to Wild Hostel Wilpattu

Experience eco-luxury living in the heart of Sri Lanka’s wilderness.

Wild Hostel Wilpattu, set at the edge of Wilpattu National Park, offers the perfect mix of comfort and adventure. Stay close to nature while enjoying modern luxury in Sri Lanka’s wild heart.

  • Comfortable rooms designed with rustic charm & modern amenities
  • Eco-friendly lodge surrounded by breathtaking nature
  • Close proximity to Wilpattu National Park for unforgettable safaris
  • Authentic Sri Lankan cuisine with fresh, local flavors
